How Much Should You Feed Your Cat or Dog?

Feeding your pet the right amount is one of the most important things you can do for their long-term health. This free cat and dog food calculator helps estimate daily food needs, kibble, wet food, and water.

What This Calculator Uses

This calculator uses a Resting Energy Rate estimate adjusted for your pet’s life stage, breed type, sex, activity level, and weight goal.

Cat Feeding Guide

Indoor neutered cats usually need fewer calories than outdoor or unneutered cats. Always adjust based on your cat’s body condition.

Dog Feeding Guide

Dogs vary widely by size and activity level. Always adjust based on your dog’s body condition and your vet’s guidance.
